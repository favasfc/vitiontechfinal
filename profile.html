Profile MEMCO Group

Profile

Founded in 1990 memco engineering Co. LLC (memco) is the parent company in our group. We are specialized in the design and planning of facilities for commercial offices, corporate, aviation, health, education, transport, retail, residential, hospitality, sport and leisure, telecommunications and commercial clients in the private and public sectors.

We operate all over the emirates namely Abu Dhabi, Dubai, Al ain and the northern Emirates.

Through this network, we service our clients regionally.
